# Pandoc Preview Editor Requirements Packet

This wiki contains the key documents for the pandoc-preview editor’s requirements and development philosophy. It is derived from the requirements packet delivered on June 7, 2026.

## Documents

- [[Pandoc-Preview-Editor-Requirements]] – user-facing and architectural requirements, restricted to non-standard app-defining behaviour.
- [[Design-Commitments-and-Development-Philosophy]] – development philosophy, threat model, ownership boundaries, and anti-slop constraints.
- [[Proof-Obligations-and-Test-Philosophy]] – what real tests must prove, including Tauri proof boundaries and banned test shapes.
- [[Feature-Disposition-Matrix]] – active requirements, non-goals, dropped features, and standard behaviours intentionally omitted from detailed specification.
